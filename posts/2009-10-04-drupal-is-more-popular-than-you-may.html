Drupal is arguably the best Content Management System (CMS) available for free, it may not be as popular as Joomla mainly because of a steep learning curve but it is a solid system with lots of features out of the box. More features can be added on in the forms of modules and look and feel can be changed in the form of themes in Drupal.


Drupal has multiplied its users, audience and developers in the past years. In every Drupal Con we hear Dries Buytaert telling that he hand not anticipated the exponential growth and how everything has come to place with all the community members.
